Life's Journey: From your first steps to your new beginning



Like many parents, when you were born, we began a journey full of firsts, of shared learning, of hugs that healed and looks that spoke volumes.

Thanks to that moment I had a new nickname, 'mum', thanks to that moment I discovered a new way of loving, thanks to that moment your father and I started a beautiful family...

Time, almost without asking permission, went by, and stage after stage, we saw you grow, evolve and dream...

Until suddenly, you showed us a ring.

Time stood still. For an instant we didn't know what to say...

Today, seeing you here, so in love, so determined, so full of illusion, we feel that our pride does not fit in our chest...

Today you are starting a new stage...

Finally, I wanted these words to be with you always.

I want you to listen to me without judging me.

I want you to express your opinion without imposing me.

I want you to trust me without demanding me.

I want you to help me without deciding for me.

Because true love does not bind, it accompanies. And when it accompanies with the soul, everything flourishes.

Ana Solano, bride's mother
